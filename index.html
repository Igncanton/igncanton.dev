ABOUT ME

My name is Ignacio Canton, I'm a self-taught web developer from Argentina who is passionate about technologies and how they help us enhance our daily lifes, from how we communicate to how we learn, how we view the world and how we connect with it and everything in between.


I'm a strong believer that things are changing in the right direction and it's becoming possible thanks to this hiper connectivity.


Everyday I learn and build a bit more of what it is my grain of sand within that future and this present.


None of this things could have been accomplished withouth the help of everyone involved in every little step of the process, and I believe it's the blueprint of how things should be done, with passionate and collaborative teams.
